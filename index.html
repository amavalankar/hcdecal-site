Our Human-Centered Design DeCal is an introductory class to design theory and practice, with a focus on human-centered design.

2 Units P/NP

Mondays, 7-9 PM

Jacobs 310

Apply by 1/12!

Students learn about the human-centered design process and theory, and engage in hands-on activities during class and in homework assignments. Throughout the semester, students will use industry standard tools to solve a problem they care about in their midterm and final project. Apps due January 20th.
